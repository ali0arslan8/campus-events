Joining Process

  1. Select a Club: Visit the Categories page to find a club that matches your interest.
  2. Fill Registration: Visit the student services desk or use the online portal link.
  3. Attend Orientation: Meet the team leads and learn about meeting schedules.
  4. Get Involved: Start participating in weekly workshops and events.

Note: Most clubs require a minimum CGPA of 2.5 for executive roles.
